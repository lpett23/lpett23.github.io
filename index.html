English at Cavendish Road State High School

English is central to the learning and development of all students. Here at Cavendish Road SHS, English strives to engage students both imaginatively and critically with literature.

    ESSENTIAL ENGLISH
  • Essential English develops and refines studentsâ€™ understanding of language, literature and literacy to enable them to interact confidently and effectively with others in everyday, community and social contexts.
    • GENERAL ENGLISH
  • General English focuses on the study of both literary texts and non-literary texts, developing students as independent, innovative and creative learners and thinkers who appreciate the aesthetic use of language, analyse perspectives and evidence, and challenge ideas and interpretations through the analysis and creation of varied texts.
